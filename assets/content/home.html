Welcome

Welcome to the DCV-ROOD statistics website, a cross-validation framework proposed for out-of-distribution (OOD) problems. This site accompanies the paper DCV-ROOD Evaluation Framework: Dual Cross-Validation for Robust Out-of-Distribution Detection. Use the sidebar to explore the different sections of the experimental results:

  • Datasets: Overview of the datasets used in the experiments.
  • Metrics: Explanation of the evaluation metrics employed.
  • Algorithms: Description of the OOD detection methods evaluated.

  • Benchmark Truth: Mean benchmark results over 100 iterations, used as the reference to evaluate DCV-ROOD.
  • Wilcoxon p-values: Pairwise statistical test results indicating significant differences between methods in the benchmark truth.
  • Hit Rate: Average number of times DCV-ROOD correctly replicated significant differences in the benchmark truth.
  • By-metric views: Easily check the results of all the experiments for a single metric in the tabs in this view.
  • Results summary: Average correct and false detections in DCV-ROOD compared to the benchmark truth
  • Significative differences: Statistically significant differences between DCV-ROOD iterations and the benchmark truth per method.

Please choose a section in the sidebar.
